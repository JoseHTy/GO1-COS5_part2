Job title: IT Support Technician

  • Position Ref: ITST01
  • Location: Melbourne, Australia
  • Job type: Full-time / Part-time
  • Salary Range: AUD $45,000 – $65,000
  • Reports To: IT Manager

About the Role:

As an IT Support Technician, you will be the first point of contact for technical support within the organization. You’ll help diagnose, troubleshoot, and resolve hardware and software issues while ensuring optimal performance of IT systems. This is a key role for maintaining productivity and smooth IT operations in a fast-paced environment.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Respond to support requests via phone, email, or in-person.
  • Diagnose and troubleshoot hardware, software, and network-related issues.
  • Install and configure computer systems, printers, and other peripherals.
  • Set up new user accounts and manage access permissions.
  • Maintain inventory of IT equipment and software licenses.
  • Provide guidance and training to users on basic IT processes.
  • Document technical issues and solutions for future reference.

Required Skills & Qualifications

  1. Strong knowledge of Windows and macOS operating systems.
  2. Familiarity with Microsoft Office Suite, networking concepts, and common hardware.
  3. Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
  4. Ability to manage multiple tasks and prioritize efficiently.
  5. Basic understanding of cybersecurity principles
  6. Bachelor’s degree (or currently pursuing) in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.

Our Values

  • Integrity

  • Team Collaboration

Job title: Junior Network Administrator

  • Position Ref: JNA12
  • Location: Sydney, Australia
  • Job Type: Full-time / Internship
  • Salary Range: AUD $50,000 – $65,000
  • Reports To: Senior Network Engineer

About the role:

As a Junior Network Administrator, you will support the installation, configuration, and maintenance of the organization's network infrastructure. You will assist in monitoring systems, identifying bottlenecks, and resolving issues to ensure optimal network performance and security.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist with network setup, including routers, switches, and firewalls.
  • Monitor network performance and troubleshoot connectivity issues.
  • Implement basic network security measures and report threats.
  • Provide technical support for users related to network access.
  • Maintain documentation of network configurations and procedures.
  • Collaborate with the IT team to ensure smooth operations.

Required Skills & Qualifications

  1. Basic understanding of networking protocols (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP).
  2. Familiarity with Cisco or Juniper network devices is a plus.
  3. Strong problem-solving and communication skills.
  4. Ability to work both independently and in a team.
  5. Currently pursuing or completed a diploma/degree in Networking, Information Technology, or related field.

Why Join Us?

Work in a collaborative, supportive team environment. Opportunity to grow your IT career and gain hands-on experience. Access to training resources and certifications. Competitive pay and flexible hours for students.
